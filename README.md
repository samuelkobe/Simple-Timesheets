# Simple Timesheets

> Lightweight time tracking for freelancers and contractors — free, open source, and privacy-first.

---

## Features

- **Projects** with hourly rates, currencies, and color coding
- **Timer** — start/stop from the popup, keeps running when you close it
- **Manual time entry** — log time after the fact in H:MM format
- **¼ hour rounding** — per-project toggle that rounds entries up to the nearest 15 minutes
- **Memos** — add a note to every timer or manual entry
- **Timesheets** — full ledger view grouped by date, filterable by project, with inline edit and delete
- **Reports** — weekly, monthly, annual, or custom date range with hours and earnings
- **CSV export** — export any report with per-project breakdowns and subtotals
- **Google Drive sync** — optional, stores data in your own Drive (we never see it)
- **Export / Import JSON** — manual backup and restore, no account needed

## Privacy

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable Google Drive sync — and even then, it goes directly to your own Google account, not ours. No servers. No analytics. No tracking.

[Read the full privacy policy](https://samuelkobe.github.io/Simple-Timesheets/privacy-policy.html)
